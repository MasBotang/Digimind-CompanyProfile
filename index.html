About Us

PT. DIGIMIND is a digital solutions development company based in Bekasi, founded in 2024, and dedicated to delivering high-quality applications across various platforms – web, desktop, and mobile.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence allows us to provide solutions that not only meet our clients' needs but also exceed their expectations. By leveraging a wide range of technologies, we deliver applications that are functional, efficient, and future-ready.
